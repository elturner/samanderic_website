Transportation


We've scheduled two departures from Hotel Durant on our wedding day. The first will be at 5:40 PM, and the second will be at 6:10 PM.

Both will depart from the front of the hotel and take you directly to the Lawrence Hall of Science, so if you're interested in taking in the views of the Bay Area or exploring the exhibits some before the ceremony, we recommend the early bus!

There will also be multiple trips back to the hotel later in the evening to accommodate people interested in leaving earlier and those who'd prefer to stay until the end of the party.

These buses are available for anyone who doesn't want to drive up to the venue, not just guests of the Hotel Durant.

Parking is available in the lot immediately north of the Hall's entrance. Parking is free for wedding guests, but vehicles left overnight will be towed by the University of California.
