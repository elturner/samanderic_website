The Lawrence Hall of Science is located in the Berkeley hills above the UC Berkeley campus at 1 Centennial Drive, Berkeley, CA. It can be reached via car, taxi, or bus from Downtown Berkeley. It is up a very substantial hill, so walking and cycling from downtown are not for the faint of heart.



Parking is available in the lot immediately north of the Hall's entrance. Parking is free for wedding guests, but vehicles left overnight will be towed by the University of California.
